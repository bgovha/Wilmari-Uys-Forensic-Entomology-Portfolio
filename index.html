About Me

Dedicated entomologist with a strong background in insect ecology, taxonomy, and development. With nearly 10 years of field and laboratory experience, I specialise in studying insect behaviour and biology to support the advancement of practical solutions to complex scientific challenges.

Passionate about science education and public engagement, I am committed to mentoring and empowering students, training professionals, and expanding entomological literacy within academia and among broader audiences. I bring a collaborative spirit, curiosity-driven mindset, and a meticulous approach to every project.
